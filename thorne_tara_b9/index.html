Welcome to Drestrier Stables

Hello, and welcome to Drestrier Stables! I'm the owner Tara. I hope you enjoy your visit and I can help you with whatever you need. Let me start by telling to you a little about us. Our stable colors are Blue and Cream. Here at Drestrier Stables we are a private stable that specialize in Andalusians, Friesians, and Lusitanos that are trained in Dressage.

Beautifully designed, and landscaped Drestrier Stables is a private stable located in Chesapeake City, Maryland on fifty acres. We have a twenty five stall climate controlled stable; each stall is twelve feet x twelve feet with Dutch style doors leading to the outside long with a sliding wooden door inside the barn. There are also fully equipped washstalls, a large tackroom and feed room. Our riding facilities include an olympic sized covered areana and a large sized outdoor areana, both setup for Dressage with synthetic and sand Mix footing, and a judge's booth. For our horses' relaxtion we have fifteen acres worth of paddocks and pastures of various sizes with smaller paddocks with fifteen gallon water troughs and the larger pastures have automatic waterers that are heated to prevent freezing in the winter.
