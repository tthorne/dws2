How Much Does It Cost?

Pennsic Costs

These are the amounts due at the gate upon arrival and covers from when you arrive until the end of Pennsic. The amount depends on whether or not you prepaid, how many weeks you prepaid for (if applicable), arrival date (first week or second week) and whether or not you are an SCA member.

If you are an adult member of the SCA at the time of check-in then you get a $25 discount.

The additional $40 paid to register for 2 weeks is NON-REFUNDABLE. You will not be refunded this amount if you decide to only come for 1 week.
